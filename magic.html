How to Play

Take Turns

Magic is more fun with friends, be kind and thoughtful as you plot to destroy your opponents.

Throw Down Some Cards

Magic is all about who has the best premium cardboard rectangles. Go to your local game store and buy as many shiny cards as you can fit in your arms.

Or Make Proxies

If you don't want to pay for magic cards... just print them out! Put them in card sleeves in front of real magic cards and no one will notice.

Magic The Gathering

Do you like pulling rabbits out of hats? Or fooling your friends with fancy card tricks? Well this is the wrong website for you then. Magic the Gathering doesn't have anything to do with magic tricks, MTG is a trading card game.

Magic is very similar to chess, poker, Monopoly, and any other game that involves a mixture of luck and skill. Alright, Monopoly is a bad example because it doesn't involve any skill, but still, you get the point. Magic is arguably one of the most difficult games to master, because it involves about 25,000 different cards

If you're interested in Magic the Gathering, you came to the right place. I made this website so that you could read it, and maybe use it to know that Magic is a card game.

Johnathan Smith

How to Play

Take Turns

Magic is more fun with friends, be kind and thoughtful as you plot to destroy your opponents.

Throw Down Some Cards

Magic is all about who has the best premium cardboard rectangles. Go to your local game store and buy as many shiny cards as you can fit in your arms.

Or Make Proxies

If you don't want to pay for magic cards... just print them out! Put them in card sleeves in front of real magic cards and no one will notice.
